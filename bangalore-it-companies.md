## Why most of this list isn't auto-scannable

The career-ops scanner ([`scan.mjs`](scan.mjs)) reads **Greenhouse / Ashby / Lever** APIs only (zero-token).
Probing all 430 companies against those APIs returned only **4 genuine matches** — the rest of the "hits" were
slug collisions with unrelated companies (e.g. `greenhouse/tcs` = a UK clinic chain, not Tata; `lever/finch` = the
fintech, not "Finch Software"). Structurally, this list is old: most firms are **defunct or acquired**, and the big
survivors (Infosys, Wipro, TCS, HCL, Cognizant, Oracle, IBM, Microsoft…) use Workday/SuccessFactors/custom portals
the zero-token scanner can't hit. So: **4 companies are auto-scannable**; the rest are **apply-manually** links below.
