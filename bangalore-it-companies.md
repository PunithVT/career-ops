## Why almost none of this is auto-scannable

The career-ops scanner ([`scan.mjs`](scan.mjs)) reads **Greenhouse / Ashby / Lever** APIs only (zero-token). Probing all 430
against those APIs + a 326-agent research pass found only **4 companies on a scannable board**. The survivors overwhelmingly use
**Workday / SuccessFactors / iCIMS / custom** portals the zero-token scanner can't hit (e.g. Infosys, Wipro, TCS, HCL, Cognizant,
Oracle, IBM, Microsoft). Most other rows are **defunct or acquired** (Satyam→Tech Mahindra, i-flex→Oracle FSS, Aztec→LTIMindtree,
iGate/Patni/Aricent→Capgemini). So: **4 auto-scannable**; the rest are **apply-manually** links below.
